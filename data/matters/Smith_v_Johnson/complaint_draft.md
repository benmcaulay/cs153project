# Draft Notes — Complaint

Case caption: Margaret A. Smith v. Robert T. Johnson
Court: Circuit Court of Hale County, Illinois
Case Number: 2024-L-00231

Count I: Negligence. Defendant Robert T. Johnson owed a duty of reasonable care
to other users of the roadway, including pedestrians lawfully within a marked
crosswalk. Defendant breached that duty by failing to stop at a red traffic
signal on January 14, 2024.

The incident occurred at the intersection of 5th Street and Marquette Boulevard,
Riverton, Illinois at approximately 6:45 p.m.

Plaintiff demands judgment in an amount in excess of $50,000, plus costs.
